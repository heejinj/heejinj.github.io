    Ayonga Hereid

    I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow at The University of Michigan in Ann Arbor, working with Prof. Jessy Grizzle. I received my Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from Georgia Institute of Technology in 2016.

    My current research focuses on developing elegant and principled control solutions for the next-generation bipedal robot and exoskeleton using computationally-efficient trajectory optimization and machine learning.
