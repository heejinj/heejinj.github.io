    Resarch Highlights

    Exoskeleton

    Collaborating with Wandercraft, a French company that builds powered exoskeletons, I am currently working on developing robustly stable, principled control solutions for a powered lower-limb exoskeleton, ATALENTE, to help person with paraplegia to walk without the use of crunches.

    Dynamic Locomotion Gait Optimization

    The focus of this project was on developing a computationally efficient and reliable gait synthesis algorithm that is scalable to high-dimensional (more than 20 DoF) humanoids.

    Online Adaptive Motion Planning for Bipeds

    Improved the performance of dynamic gait optimization so as to realize adaptive online motion planning for bipedal robots.

    Energy Efficient Locomotion at DARPA Robotic Challenges (DRC)

    .

    Dynamic Legged Locomotion via Formal Control Methods

    Utilize formal control methods to realize dynamic walking on highly underactuated bipeds.

    Networked Instrument Bus Protocol

    Aiming at the deficiencies of the popular instrument buses, we designed a custom networked communication protocol for high-performance instrumentation system based on FPGA and M-LVDS techniques.
