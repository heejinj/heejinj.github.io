    Networked Instrument Bus Protocol

    Abstract

    Many existing instrument buses cannot simultaneously satisfy the request of multi-hosts communication, high data transmit rate, timely transmission and simple connection interface. Aiming at the deficiencies of the popular instrument buses, we designed a new networked instrument bus. This new design include two independent media access channel, 8-bit width parallel data transmission, CSMA/BA(bitwise arbitration method) technology, as well as synchronization and trigger mplementation of networked instrument bus. The signal-integrity design of high-speed digital serial bus hardware is designed to realize high-speed communication. All aspects of bus protocol, including layer definition, frame structure, bus timing and acknowledgment, frame filter, error detection etc, are introduced in terms of a complete framework. Further, IEEE-1588 based precise time synchronization protocol is implemented within networked instrument bus to realized the real-time synchronization among agents. The proposed framework was then applied on a real-time data acquisition and control platform for user to test and troubleshoot digital/analog signals within a complicated system.
